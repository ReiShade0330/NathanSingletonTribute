Nathan Earl Singleton

1976 - 2019

" Even when things get hard, you have to do it anyway. "

Biography

Nathan Earl Singleton was born January 25th, 1976. He was born in Denver Colorado and lived with his mother, Rita, and his sister, Rayan. Many loved him for how honest he was, about himself and others, and got along with him well. He married Jessica Arthur and had two kids with her, later on getting divorced. Him and his two kids, Sarah and Harold Singleton, lived in many places, but they always had and loved each other. Eventually, he fell in love with Tiffany Jones and moved down to Tulsa Oklahoma with his kids, and died later on the day of May 6th, 2019.
